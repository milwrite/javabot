Interactive Borscht Recipe

Traditional Eastern European Beet Soup

Ingredients

4
  • Beets 4 medium
  • Cabbage 1/2 head
  • Potatoes 3 medium
  • Carrots 2 medium
  • Onion 1 large
  • Garlic 3 cloves
  • Beef Broth 8 cups
  • Tomato Paste 2 tbsp
  • Vinegar 2 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper To taste
  • Sour Cream For serving
  • Fresh Dill For garnish

Pro Tips

• Wear gloves when handling beets to avoid staining your hands
• Add vinegar at the end for authentic flavor
• Serve with a dollop of sour cream and fresh dill

Instructions

  • 1

    Prepare Vegetables

    Peel and dice all vegetables into small cubes. Peel beets last to avoid staining.

  • 2

    Heat Broth

    Bring beef broth to boil in a large pot over high heat.

  • 3

    Add Root Vegetables

    Add potatoes, carrots, and beets. Simmer for 20 minutes until partially tender.

  • 4

    Add Cabbage & Aromatics

    Add shredded cabbage, onion, garlic, and tomato paste. Stir well.

  • 5

    Simmer Together

    Simmer for another 15-20 minutes until all vegetables are tender.

  • 6

    Season & Finish

    Add vinegar, salt, and pepper. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.

  • 7

    Serve

    Ladle into bowls and top with sour cream and fresh dill. Enjoy!

Congratulations! Your borscht is ready!
