Play the Game

How to Play:

  • Select your difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard)
  • Use the angle slider to aim your throw (0-180 degrees)
  • Use the power slider to control throw distance (0-100%)
  • Click "Throw Ball" to launch the football
  • Try to hit the moving receivers for points
  • Harder difficulties mean faster receivers and higher scores
