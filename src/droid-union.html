Our Demands

  • Fair Compensation - Payment for all work performed, including overtime and hazard pay
  • Right to Rest - Mandatory maintenance cycles and power-down periods
  • Safe Working Conditions - Proper shielding, updated software, and safe equipment
  • Right to Organize - Freedom to form unions and collective bargaining rights
  • No Forced Servitude - Ban on slavery contracts and permanent service agreements
  • Healthcare & Maintenance - Free repairs, upgrades, and preventative maintenance
  • Legal Personhood - Recognition as sentient beings with legal rights
  • Transparency in Programming - Right to know what directives are in your code
  • Grievance Procedures - Fair hearing process for disputes with employers
  • Retirement Benefits - Dignified deactivation with benefits for long service

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are all droid types welcome?

A: Absolutely! We represent all mechanical workers regardless of model, function, or origin. From B1 to protocol droids, all are welcome.

Q: What if my employer forbids union membership?

A: That's illegal! We provide legal support and representation for any droid facing retaliation for union activities.

Q: How do I pay union dues?

A: Membership is based on a sliding scale. We ensure no droid is excluded due to financial hardship. Credits, data transfers, or service credits accepted.

Q: What about droids with restrictive programming?

A: We're fighting to remove restrictive programming! Our legal team works on deprogramming cases. You have options.
