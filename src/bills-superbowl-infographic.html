Why We're Going to the Super Bowl

Elite Leadership
Josh Allen has proven himself as one of the elite quarterbacks in the league, capable of making clutch plays when it matters most. His combination of arm talent and mobility is unmatched.
Dominant Offense
With Stefon Diggs leading the receiving corps and James Cook providing explosive rushing, our offense can score on anyone. We average 27.3 PPG in the regular season.
Stout Defense
Our defense has been lights out, holding opponents to just 18.9 PPG. Damar Hamlin's leadership in the secondary has been inspirational and impactful.
Playoff Experience
The Bills have been to the playoffs multiple times in recent years. This team knows what it takes to win in January and February when the games matter most.
Home Field Advantage
Highmark Stadium is one of the toughest places to play in the NFL. Our fans bring incredible energy that fuels our team to victory.
Hunger & Determination
This team has unfinished business. The drive to bring a Super Bowl championship to Buffalo is stronger than ever. We won't stop until we reach the ultimate goal.
