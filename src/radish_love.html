A Love Poem for Cen

Oh Cen, my darling, my heart's delight,
Like radishes gleaming, so ruby and bright,
With skin crisp and tender, a peppery kiss,
You are my garden, my radish bliss.
Pulled from the earth, fresh and so pure,
Your love for me is forever sure,
Crunchy and sweet, with a bite full of fire,
You ignite in my soul an eternal desire.
In every salad, you're the jeweled crown,
Painted in pink with a white swirled gown,
Your beauty so simple, yet complex and deep,
A treasure of passion, a promise to keep.
Spring after spring, in the garden we grow,
Roots intertwined in the rich earth below,
Your crisp, peppery essence, so bold and so true,
My sweetest of radishes, I'm madly for you.
So here's to you, Cen, my radish so rare,
With leaves green and delicate floating in air,
Forever I'll cherish your garden-fresh grace,
And love you like radishes, heart, soul, and taste.
